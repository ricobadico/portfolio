About me

Hi, and thanks for visiting! I'm a full stack developer who likes to suss out complexity, contribute everywhere, and bolster the team.

I began coding in the school of FIOFY (Figure It Out For Yourself). After completing my formal training at the top of my class, I hit the ground running, learning and doing at work with a fiesty, eager thoroughness that hasn't left me since.

A few years on in the industry, I've been able to distinguish myself as a thorough contributor for both requirements and development, a support to new and even more seasoned teammates, and a writer of code that is both clear and clever (in that order).

I have a backend lean with a focus in .NET and SQL, but am always happy to apply my strong logical foundation to a new framework.

I have experience in teaching and training, which helps shape the structure and communication skills I bring to the job. I also lost a few years of my life to an injury, and looking back after recovery, gained invaluable lessons in making the most of my time, and continuously learning and giving.

I believe you'll find me a quick-learning, hard-working, and downright pleasant.
